MID-AUTUMN 2012 FESTIVAL IN EMERALDA

In Vietnam, Têt-Trung-Thu or the Mid-Autumn Festival is one of the most popular family holidays. It is held on the 15th day of the 8th lunar month.


Welcome to one of the world’s great celebrations – Emeralda Mid-Autumn Festival ! The ancient Vietnamese harvest festival is given a modern treatment here in Asia’s world city, where glowing lanterns merge with the city’s trademark neon, fiery dragons dance through the busy streets and a full moon shines down on a festive metropolis.


Delightedly celebrate Mid Autumn Festival 2012 with hundreds of delicious and original moon cakes from Vietnamese greatly reputatable brand-name as Kinh Do, Nhu Lan, Bibica, Dai Phat... at Organics Restaurant.


Experience the passion of Vietnamese mythology in the glow of an innovative lantern structure. Feel the fire and fury of an ancient ritual on the streets of a modern metropolis. You also can take a chance to join the "FESTIVAL SENDS LANTERNS TOWARD HEAVEN" and enjoy this timeless Vietnamese celebration in a uniquely Emeralda way, with festival foods – traditional and nouveau – and moonlight cruises in spectacular Emeralda Resort Ninh Binh.
