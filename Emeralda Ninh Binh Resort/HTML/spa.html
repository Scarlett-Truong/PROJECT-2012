The spa La Cochinchine offers a total body purifier package and a wide range of therapies and pampering, including Asian and Vietnamese specialties, facial and body massages, exotic body exfoliation and wraps, aromatherapy, sauna and steam. The comprehensive menu of treatments and pampering is administered by skilled, caring therapists. There are daily classes of yoga, A central swimming pool offers a great place to relax before, after, or as an alternative to sightseeing, and a fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment is ideal for a rejuvenating workout after a day.
